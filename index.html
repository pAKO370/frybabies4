FRYbabies

"this is how we roll"


Established in 2010, FRYbabies is a vintage mobile food unit that prides itself on serving a variety of sandwiches fresh from the grill, deep fried sweets and treats and hand crafted beverages. To experience our culinary creations, look for us at area fairs, festivals, auctions, farmers markets and special events. We look forward to seeing you!
